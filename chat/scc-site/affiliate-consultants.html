Affiliate Consultancy

Affiliate Consultancy, a branch of Small College Consulting, serves as a bridge between small colleges and a network of seasoned professionals, each adept at addressing the unique challenges faced by your institution. Our carefully selected consultants possess specialized expertise across essential areas, such as strategic enrollment, leadership development, accreditation planning, athletics, finance, and philanthropic efforts. A key attribute of our affiliates is that they operate as full-time consultants and are not currently affiliated with any campus, thereby eliminating conflicts of interest and ensuring their unwavering commitment to your institution's success. Each affiliate is well-acquainted with the complexities of small college operations, offering guidance on pricing optimization, financial aid enhancements, executive searches, and improved organizational design.


With Affiliate Consultancy, you unlock a resource of experts who grasp the intricacies of small colleges and are dedicated to effecting meaningful change. Whether your aim is to boost student engagement, enhance financial stability, or refine strategic planning, we connect you with the ideal consultant to help realize your objectives and promote your institution's ongoing success. Allow us to assist you in navigating the challenges of today while paving the way for a brighter future for your college.
