Future Options Assessment

Guiding Institutions Through Strategic Choices: Equipping Small Colleges and Acquiring Institutions to Make Informed, Mission-Driven Decisions About Their Future.


The Challenge


Small colleges across the country face unprecedented pressures: shrinking enrollments, rising costs, demographic shifts, and intensifying competition. Many institutions find themselves with limited financial runway, forcing presidents and boards to confront high-stakes decisions without the full picture. Too often, action comes late, and closure feels like the only option.


For acquiring institutions, the risks are equally high. A poorly executed merger or acquisition can strain finances, dilute brand identity, or create mission misalignment. Both sides need clarity, structure, and discipline to make choices that strengthen, rather than weaken, long-term stability.

Future Options Assessment

The Future Options Assessment is a confidential, structured, expert-led process that provides a comprehensive, 360-degree view of potential pathways forward. The goal is not to push any single outcome, but to equip leadership with the analysis, insights, and perspective needed to make informed, strategic decisions.


What the assessment includes:


  • Merger, Partnership, and Strategic Alliance Exploration: SWOT analysis, cultural fit review, synergy valuation, and identification of potential partners.


  • Financial Impact Modeling: Side-by-side comparisons of merger, acquisition, alliance, status quo, and even closure scenarios, including costs, synergies, and long-term ROI.


  • Student-Centered Review: Evaluating how each pathway affects access, stability, and long-term student success.


  • Operational Assessment & Triage: Immediate support in areas such as enrollment, marketing, finance, and HR to extend financial runway, combined with long-term integration planning.


  • Mission Alignment: Ensuring any recommended pathway strengthens, rather than dilutes, the institution’s identity and values.


Our Team & Tools


The assessment is delivered by SCC’s deep bench of experts: former college presidents, CFOs, enrollment leaders, and specialists in legal and HR. To complement this expertise, we integrate advanced diagnostic tools such as Perspective Data Science’s proprietary dashboard, which provides unmatched visibility into institutional financial health and operational sustainability.


Why SCC?


Unlike firms that simply “broker” mergers, SCC brings a holistic, student-first approach. We don’t just explore mergers; we stabilize immediate challenges, evaluate multiple options, and ensure that your mission remains at the center. The result is a process that is pragmatic, data-driven, and respectful of the identity your institution has built.


Confidentiality


SCC recognizes the sensitivity of the information shared during the Future Options Assessment process. All materials, data, and discussions provided by the interested institution are treated as strictly confidential. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and integrity of institutional information throughout the engagement. SCC is fully prepared to enter into Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) as needed to protect both parties and ensure a secure, trust-based collaboration.



The Outcome


The Future Options Assessment delivers:

  • Clarity on every possible path forward and the strengths and risks of each.
  • Data-informed insights to guide boardroom and leadership decisions.
  • Confidence that leadership has fully evaluated cultural, financial, and mission alignment.


SCC’s mission is simple: to help colleges pursue futures that are strategic, sustainable, and mission-driven. Whether that means merger, partnership, acquisition, or a renewed independent path, our role is to ensure leaders move forward with clarity and confidence.
