09 / Disclosure Accordion & tabs

Revealed on request, never shouted.

Accordions carry FAQ and policy detail. Tabs let a program page switch between degree variants without a new load.

Do I need to be Catholic to apply?
No. Holy Family is a Catholic university in the Nazareth tradition, but our community includes students of many faiths and no faith. The only expectation is that you're willing to participate in a campus where those conversations happen openly.
What does the financial aid process actually look like?
File the FAFSA, then wait about two weeks for our financial aid office to send a counselor-built award letter. You can reach that same counselor by phone or email through the whole process, which is the part we're most proud of.
Can I transfer credits from another university?
Usually, yes. Submit transcripts with your application and a counselor will do a course-by-course review within five business days. Most community-college credits transfer one-for-one into the equivalent Holy Family course.
How soon after applying will I hear back?
We aim for an admissions decision within two weeks of a complete application, often faster in the early cycles. Counselors will call you when a decision is ready rather than leaving you to hunt for it in an email.

15 / Motion Motion & interaction

Lifted, never bouncy.

Almost every interactive element uses the same cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) curve and the same small lift + shadow step. The point is calm, not kinetic.

Card hover

translateY(-4px) with a step up from --shadow-sm to --shadow-md. Border color shifts from --border-soft to --border-strong. Duration 320ms.

Button press

Hover lifts the primary by 1px and deepens the yellow halo. The arrow glyph slides translateX(3px) over 300ms. No scale, no color jump.

Nav underline

A 2px yellow bar grows from scaleX(0) to scaleX(1) on hover, anchored at the left edge. Duration 280ms, ease-out.

Hero zoom

The hero photo begins at scale(1.02) and eases to scale(1.08) over 22 seconds. Slow, almost subliminal, never calling attention to itself.

Live dot pulse

The athletics “live” dot breathes from 1.0 to 1.4 and back with opacity 0.55, looped on a 2s interval. Only used for time-bound states.

Accordion open

The chevron circle rotates 180deg and fills yellow. The panel content uses the browser's default details open, so we only color-animate the trigger.

Durationsshort (180–220ms) · medium (280–320ms) · long (420–900ms, hero-only) Every transition shares var(--ease-out), so timing stays coherent across hovers and scrolls.
Design system note

A system is only useful if the team can reach it.

Every token on this page is defined once, in a single :root block, and referenced from every mockup. The point is that Holy Family's marketing team, and any future partner, can open a single page, read it in one sitting, and build a new campaign page that still looks like Holy Family.
