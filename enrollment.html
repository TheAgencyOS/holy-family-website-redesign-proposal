Where the leak happens

The current funnel has seven specific leaks. We built the site around fixing all of them.

Current state · April 2026

The site converts like it's 2019.

  • 20+ formsSeparate request-info forms fragmented across programs. No CRM routing.
  • 14.2 sMobile LCP on program pages. Google considers >4 s slow.
  • 1 CTAPer program page, buried below the fold. No pricing, no time-to-degree.
  • 0 schemaZero Schema.org JSON-LD for degrees. Invisible to ChatGPT & Perplexity.
  • 42%Of URLs (670) are old news articles competing with enrollment content for crawl.
  • 0A/B tests running. No GA4 conversion events tied to enrollment.
What we ship · Q1 2027

The site converts like it's 2027.

  • 1 smart formOne routed form. Pre-fills from the URL. Fires into Slate in 800 ms.
  • < 2.0 sTarget mobile LCP. We hit it on Cloudflare's edge every time.
  • 4 CTAsAbove the fold on every program. Price, schedule, time-to-degree, apply.
  • Full schemaEducationalOrganization, Program, Course, Person, FAQ. Visible to LLMs.
  • +400 archivedOld news moved to archive. Crawl budget flows to enrollment pages.
  • Live dashboardGA4 funnel events tied to Slate. Dashboard on day 1.

The model, honestly
Every number on this page is interactive — drag the sliders, change the model. We built this because the competition will email you a ROI slide with numbers you can't audit. Here's ours, with the full arithmetic exposed. If any of these assumptions are wrong for Holy Family, change them and we'll ship a new model on 48 hours' notice.
01Baseline inquiry rate: 1.5% of visitors (higher-ed industry average, Hanover Research 2024)
02Application start rate: 15% of RFI, conservative Catholic-private Northeast band
03Admit rate: 65%, matching HFU's published 2025 data
04Yield: 22%, the regional Catholic-private benchmark
05Tuition blended net of aid across UG and grad programs
06Year-one revenue only — the 4-year LTV figure quadruples it
07Cost engine = phase-1 discovery + build + content migration, no retainer
08Payback math divides cost by weekly revenue at the selected conversion lift
