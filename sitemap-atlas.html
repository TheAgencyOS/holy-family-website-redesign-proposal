— Small College Consulting audit methodology · April 2026
Audit methodology
Every number on this page is reproducible. We ran a 5-step AI-assisted audit on all 1,601 URLs in the Drupal index. Nothing was deleted; nothing was flagged without evidence. The full rationale log is exportable as CSV on kickoff day. Rick signs every archive batch before it ships.
01Crawl & index — full Drupal sitemap + GA4 URL list cross-referenced against live 200-status responses.
02Semantic clustering — embeddings (text-embedding-3-large) to group near-duplicate pages across programs and news.
03Duplicate detection — cosine similarity ≥ 0.90 flagged for consolidation. Forms, RFIs, and template pages collapsed.
04Freshness + GA4 traffic — modified-date < 2022 AND <50 sessions/month → archive candidate.
05Manual stakeholder review — every archive batch sent to Rick in CSV with one-click restore.
LLM processing cost~$20–30
Stakeholder review time4–6 hrs
Archive reversibility100%
