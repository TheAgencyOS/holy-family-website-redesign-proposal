Our approach · the three pillars 08 / 15
Three pillars, one engagement

Whole Person. Enrollment Intelligence. AI Migration Lab.

Every section traces back to one of three commitments. Together they are how a five-month engagement at $145,000 ships what traditional agencies charge twice as much to deliver in twice the time.

  1. I

    Whole Person Thesis — strategic anchor.

    The site reflects the formation Holy Family actually offers — academic, vocational, spiritual — without flattening it into marketing copy. Program pages, faculty profiles, and student stories are written and structured so a prospective student and their family can recognize the institution, not just receive a pitch. Mission integration sits in the main narrative, not buried in an “About” subfolder.

  2. II

    Enrollment Intelligence — conversion thesis.

    Every design and technical decision traces to a measurable enrollment outcome — RFI conversion, application starts, yield. Slate is integrated via Gravity Forms webhook on day one, not a post-launch afterthought. The 23 duplicate RFI forms collapse into one smart form with program routing. An AI Concierge for admissions ships in the base scope — the same architecture is producing 2,300+ leads at a peer institution today, with no active management.

  3. III

    AI Migration Lab — technical thesis.

    The Drupal→WordPress migration uses AI where AI is reliable (content audit, HTML cleanup, alt text, meta descriptions, semantic redirect mapping, post-migration QA) and human review where judgment matters. AI drafts, humans decide, code executes, tests verify. This is the principal reason five months is realistic.
