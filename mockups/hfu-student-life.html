How you live here

Six ways to belong. One Tiger family.

Student life at Holy Family is built on the same traditions the Sisters of the Holy Family of Nazareth brought with them in 1954 — hospitality, scholarship, and the unshakeable belief that everyone has a seat at the table.

01

Residence Life

Live where you learn. Traditional halls, suite-style apartments, and tight-knit floor communities on both Philadelphia and Newtown campuses — each with live-in RAs and 24/7 support.

Explore housing
02

Dining

A seat at every table. Flexible meal plans, late-night grab-and-go, real-food menus, and the kind of dining-hall conversations that turn into lifelong friendships.

See meal plans
03

Clubs & Activities

More than 25 student-led organizations, from the Black Student Union to Theatre Guild. If the club you want doesn't exist yet, we'll help you start it this semester.

Browse clubs
04

Campus Ministry

Daily Mass, retreats in the Pocono Mountains, service trips, and interfaith dialogue open to every student — Catholic or not. Come as you are.

Visit the chapel
05

Health & Wellness

Free counseling, on-campus health clinic, peer-led wellness workshops, and a brand-new mental-health portal. Because showing up for yourself is part of showing up for your class.

Wellness resources
06

Student Support

Advising, tutoring, career coaching, accessibility services, and first-generation programming — a web of people whose full-time job is making sure you don't walk any part of this alone.
