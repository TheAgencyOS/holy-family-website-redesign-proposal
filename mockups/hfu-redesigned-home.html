Our mission

A vibrant Catholic learning community.

Holy Family University educates the whole person for a life of integrity, service, and personal excellence. Founded in 1954 by the Sisters of the Holy Family of Nazareth, we are a four-school institution rooted in the Catholic intellectual tradition and tuned to the realities of working students in greater Philadelphia.
