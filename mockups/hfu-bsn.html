Career outcomes

Where Holy Family nurses go.

A BSN from Holy Family opens the full spectrum of registered-nursing careers — inpatient acute care, community health, advanced clinical specialties.

Graduates move into practice across the specialties that keep a health system running — ICU, emergency, OB, pediatrics, mental health — and into the community roles that reach patients outside hospital walls: school nursing, hospice, home care, and travel nursing.

For nurses who want to keep going, the BSN is the required degree to advance into Holy Family's graduate programs, including the MSN and the Doctor of Nursing Practice. Many of our alumni return to campus for the next chapter of their careers.
