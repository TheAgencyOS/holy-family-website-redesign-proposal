Our story

A vibrant Catholic learning community.

  • Holy Family University educates the whole person for a life of integrity, service, and personal excellence, in the Catholic intellectual tradition.
  • Founded in 1954 by the Sisters of the Holy Family of Nazareth and led today by President Anne M. Prisco, Ph.D.
  • Two Philadelphia campuses, four schools, and a strategic plan titled Advancing Knowledge and Transforming Lives.
