What to expect

What a visit feels like.

A Holy Family visit is low-pressure and student-led. No hard sell.

01

A student ambassador

A current HFU student walks you through the campus they live on. Ask them anything.

02

A counselor one-on-one

Your assigned admissions counselor meets with you and your family. Bring questions.

03

The real buildings

Tour residence halls, classrooms, the chapel, the simulation lab, and the dining hall. See it before you apply.

Can't wait to meet you

Book the visit. The rest will follow.
