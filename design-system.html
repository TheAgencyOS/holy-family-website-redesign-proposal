Design systems · for Holy Family University

Four directions. Each anchored to a single mathematical ratio.

Before we choose a visual language for the redesigned Holy Family site, we want the committee to see the full type, color, and component kit of each direction side by side. Every size, spacing step, and line height on this page is derived — nothing is improvised. Pick the one that sounds most like Holy Family, and we build it into a production design system during the June kickoff.
